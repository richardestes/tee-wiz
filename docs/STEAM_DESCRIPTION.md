**Tee off. Cast spells. Survive the back nine.**

Tee-Wiz is a first-person golf roguelite that hands you a club in one hand and a wizard's
staff in the other. Cart across handcrafted courses, pull up to a hole, and trigger an
encounter — then defend yourself against waves of enemies while you line up the perfect
shot. Your right hand swings a club-staff at whatever your cursor's aimed at; your left
hand auto-casts spells at the nearest threat, as long as you've got the mana to pay for
them.

Mana isn't free. Curve your ball through floating loops to bank fire, water, poison,
lightning, and dark energy, then spend it to play cards from a deck that fires
automatically, left to right. Reorder your spells on the fly, upgrade them Vampire
Survivors-style, and swap between club-staffs that change which magic you can wield. Kill
without getting hit and your combo climbs from D all the way to S — but every rank makes
the enemies hit harder and move faster. Finish a hole clean for a top grade and a better
chest, dropped straight from the sky onto the green.

Sink the cup to end any encounter instantly and clear the field. Level up for instant
rewards, magnet up the loot, customize your cart, and push deeper into the course. Die,
and the run is over. How far can you make it before the back nine takes you down?
